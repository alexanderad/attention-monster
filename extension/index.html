About

We all spend time on the web. We try to be productive and get things done, and we try to be playful and entertain ourselves. Luckily these days that's not a question.

However this is so easy to get distracted by things. Attention Monster is here to help you identify websites taking your precious time away!

Clean browsing data

  • We care about your privacy that is why your browsing history stats are only stored on your computer
  • We don't have any usage trackers, external analytics services installed alongside extension or any other cloud magic attached
  • Cleaning up stats effectively removes all the collected usage statistics and this is irreversible operation.
